About Us

Photography is the art, science, and practice of creating images by capturing light on a photosensitive surface, typically a digital sensor or photographic film. The word "photography" comes from the Greek words light and representation by means of lines or drawing, which together mean "drawing with light." Photography allows us to freeze moments in time, capture scenes, people, objects, and events, and preserve them as visual records. It is a widely used medium for various purposes, including artistic expression, journalism, advertising, documentation, and personal memories. The process of photography involves using a camera or a similar device to focus light through a lens onto the photosensitive surface.
